# 👁️ Sentinel AI Defense

## Applied vulnerability research · ML-assisted CVE triage · Red teaming

We are a small, independent research group that builds AI tooling to surface and prioritize vulnerabilities in large enterprise codebases.

We don't sell a platform. We do the research, we read the diffs, and the models help us cover ground we couldn't reach by hand.

---

### 🔬 What we do

- **AI-assisted vulnerability discovery** — RAG over AST + call graph, not just text chunks. We find the bugs humans skim past.
- **Threat intelligence** — CVE triage that actually maps to your environment. We tell you which 3 of 90 "critical" advisories matter.
- **Red teaming, with the model in the loop** — human‑driven adversarial testing, accelerated by LLMs. No autonomous agents near production.
